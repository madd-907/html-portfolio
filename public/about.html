About Me

Background

I grew up in central Illinois, on my family's corn and soybean farm. After being very active in highschool playing as many sports as I could, I attended the University of Illinois in Champaign-Urbana. I received my Bachelor's degree in Agricultural Communications with a minor in Food & Environmental Systems and an emphasis in Advertising after 3 and a half years of study. I spent a semester abroad at the University of Sydney in Australia and took various cultural studies as well as an intro to graphic design course.

Work Experience

Right after graduation, I worked in central Florida for John Bean Technologies (JBT) and performed general marketing duties as well as global lead generation. After losing my position in a company-wide restructuring, I moved back home to Illinois and took on a job with a Saas startup based out of Sydney, Australia called Neara. Having recently lost that position due to yet another restructuring, I've considered a career change due to the deep dive into website development and minor coding experience I had with Neara.
